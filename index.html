About Us

Global Expertise in Oil Field Geology

Ivan Consultings is a premier geological consulting firm specializing in oil field exploration and development. Based in Phoenix, Arizona, we serve clients across the globe with cutting-edge geological analysis and strategic insights.

Our team, led by renowned geologist Ivan Dominik, brings decades of hands-on experience in identifying promising drilling locations, assessing reservoir potential, and optimizing extraction strategies.

We pride ourselves on our track record of success, with over 200 completed oil wells across six continents, each backed by meticulous geological assessment and expert oversight.

Our Services

Exploration Geology

Comprehensive geological assessment to identify promising drilling locations and evaluate reservoir potential using advanced technologies.

Well Site Geology

On-site geological supervision during drilling operations, sample analysis, and real-time decision making to optimize well placement.

Reservoir Characterization

Detailed analysis of reservoir properties, fluid dynamics, and production potential to maximize recovery and efficiency.

CEO Profile

Ivan Dominik

With over 30 years of experience in oil field geology, Ivan Dominik has established himself as a leading expert in the industry. His career spans six continents and includes work with major energy corporations and independent operators alike.

Ivan's expertise includes basin analysis, seismic interpretation, well planning, and reservoir characterization. His innovative approaches to geological challenges have resulted in the successful development of hundreds of oil wells worldwide.

Based in Phoenix, Arizona, Ivan continues to provide strategic geological consulting to clients across the globe, combining traditional geological methods with cutting-edge technology to deliver exceptional results.
